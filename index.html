"As the sun paints the afternoon sky with its warm, golden hues, my camera captures three enchanting portraits of flowers with different poetic beauty.The first, a close-up embrace with a flower's delicate petals, reveals an intimate connection. In the second, we find ourselves amidst a small vintage vase of flower blooms by the window. Finally, the third image takes us on a journey with the rustic simplicity of flower clusters adorning boats during the festive Tet holiday in Vietnam"
